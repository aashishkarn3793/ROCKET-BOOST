# 🚀 Rocket Boost

> **Master your thrust. Navigate the obstacles. Reach the finish line.**

Rocket Boost is a physics-based arcade game developed in Unity using C#. Players control a rocket and must carefully maneuver through challenging levels filled with obstacles, narrow passages, and hazardous terrain.

The game tests precision, timing, and control as players use the rocket's thrusters to reach the destination without crashing.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

Players pilot a rocket through a series of increasingly challenging environments. Using controlled bursts of thrust, the objective is to safely navigate around obstacles and reach the landing platform to complete each level.

Success requires precise movement, patience, and mastery of the rocket's controls.
